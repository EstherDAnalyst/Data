Power BI Projects

This project is a visualization of transaction data from OList. OList is the largest department store in Brazil. The dataset has information on 99k orders made at multiple marketplaces in Brazil.

The data captured the total numbers of customers, total products, total payments made, total numbers of sellers, customer's state, Customer's payment type etc.

Insights and Recommendations:

✪ Order Overview: The Olist platform processed over 99,000 orders, This resulted in the fulfillment of over 96,000 deliveries with a total payment of 1642, involving 3,095 sellers .

✪ Payment Preferences: Credit cards were the preferred payment method, accounting for over 75% of total payments.

✪ Order Volume Trends: The highest order volume was recorded in the month of august.

✪ Delivery Success: Out of 99,000 orders, 96,000 were successfully delivered to customers.

✪ Customer Locations: Sao Paulo was the leading state for shoppers. Implementing targeted advertising can boost order numbers in states with lower volumes.

This project is a in-depth analysis of Unicom data, encompassing a total of 1,072 companies with a combined valuation of £3.71 trillion. The data spans 15 industries, 46 countries, and 257 cities, offering a comprehensive overview of the global market landscape.

Insights and Recommendations:

✪ Industry Insights: The Fintech industry recorded the highest total number of companies, indicating a robust interest and growth in financial technology solutions. Investors should consider increasing their stakes in the fintech sector, given its high growth potential and market demand.

✪ Monthly Valuation Trends: April recorded the highest valuation at £0.46 trillion, highlighting a period of significant financial activity and investment. Monitoring valuation trends can help identify optimal investment periods, such as the notable peak in April.

✪ Geographical Distribution: he United States leads in terms of valuation, with a staggering £1.9 trillion, showcasing its dominance in the global market.Zhuhai emerged as the city with the highest valuation at £5.0 billion, reflecting its strategic importance and economic growth.


Companies looking to expand internationally should consider establishing a presence in the United States to leverage its high valuation market. Exploring opportunities in cities like Zhuhai can provide access to burgeoning economic zones with significant growth prospects.

This project is a visualization of data analysis of WHO COVID-19 cases globally, with 775 new cases reported across 240 countries, accumulating to a total of 88 billion cases, and with 7 million new deaths recorded, totaling 1 billion cumulative deaths, it's evident that the pandemic continues to present significant challenges worldwide.

Recommendations:

✪ Vaccination Campaigns: Prioritize the acceleration of vaccination efforts globally to achieve higher levels of immunity and mitigate the spread of the virus.

✪ Public Health Measures: Reinforce the importance of wearing masks, practicing social distancing, and maintaining good hygiene practices to limit transmission.

✪ Enhanced Surveillance: Strengthen surveillance systems to promptly identify and respond to outbreaks, ensuring timely intervention and containment measures.

✪ Global Collaboration: Foster international cooperation and solidarity to address disparities in vaccine distribution, support healthcare systems in vulnerable regions, and facilitate the sharing of resources and expertise.

✪ Risk Communication: Improve communication strategies to combat misinformation and promote vaccine acceptance, empowering individuals to make informed decisions about their health.

By implementing these recommendations and maintaining a collective effort, we can strive towards controlling the spread of COVID-19 and mitigating its impact on global health and well-being.
